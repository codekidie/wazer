About

The objective of this app is to provide an android application career assessment system for Filipinos. Specifically, this app aims:

  • 1. To develop a career assessment system through android application that can help student decide which career to pursue.
  • 2. To develop a system that is visually appealing to the students that one will be tempted to complete the test.
  • 3. To develop a career assessment test that will be convenient and more accessible to every students.
